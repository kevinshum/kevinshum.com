About

Born in California, I've lived, worked, and traveled across 5 continents. I studied computer science and graduated with honours from MIT. I've developed next-generation human-computer interfaces and experiences in acaedmia, industry, and startups — including the MIT Media Lab, CSAIL, and ETH Zürich. I've also worked on cloud computing at Microsoft and blogged for MIT Admissions.

I'm a former Team USA figure skater and have two collegiate national titles. Check out my features on MIT News, Diablo Magazine, and Squarespace — and how about Kevin Shum Day?

Interested in the Seattle real estate market? I'm a second-generation Coldwell Banker licensed realtor.
