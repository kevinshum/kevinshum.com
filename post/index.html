Profile

Kevin Shum — studied computer science and engineering at MIT and graduated with honours, Eta Kappa Nu and Tau Beta Pi. He specialised in human-computer interaction in his research at the MIT Media Lab, CSAIL, ETH Zürich, and various startups. He also taught STEM in schools around the world with MIT's international education program.

Shum is a former Team USA figure skater and has traveled across five continents as an athlete and scholar. He has been featured on MIT News, Diablo Magazine, and Squarespace, and even has a day named after him.

He currently lives in Seattle and builds infrastructure tools for the Azure cloud computing platform. Follow his journey on his blog. Drop him a note!
