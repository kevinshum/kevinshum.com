02 Implementation

Role

I designed and laser cut the acryllic, bent it into the touchpad shape, and wired in LEDs with a USB cable and a on/off switch.

I used OpenCV to track finger movements through the camera at the base of the touchpad, and wrote the algorithms to detect different gestures and adapt the music player accordingly.
