KS

A California native, Kevin Shum studies Computer Science at MIT, with a concentration in Theater Arts. He specializes in the intersection of design and engineering, and has worked with startups, the MIT Media Lab, and Microsoft.


Shum has traveled to over 26 countries and counting — revising Informatik at ETH Zürich, Switzerland, teaching high school students in Germany, and competing on Team USA.

He is the two-time reigning US Figure Skating Collegiate Champion, and writes for MIT Admissions. Check out his feature in an ad campaign, and a day named after him.
