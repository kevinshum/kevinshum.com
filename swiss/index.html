About →

A California native, Kevin Shum studies computer science and the arts at MIT. He specializes in the intersection of design and engineering, developing the next-generation of user interfaces, experiences, and interactions.

He has visited over 26 countries — and counting — studying abroad at ETH Zürich, teaching high school students in Germany, and competing around the US and internationally on Team USA.

Shum is the two-time reigning US Figure Skating Collegiate Champion. Throughout his travels, he enjoys photography and writes about his adventures for MIT Admissions.
